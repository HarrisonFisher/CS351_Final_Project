Directions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente, according to package instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the saucepan and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  4. Simmer the tomato sauce for about 10 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the tomato sauce. Toss to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.
  6. Serve the spaghetti hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
